Frequently Asked Questions

Where are you located? +

Agrinexa is an online platform that breach the gap between farm producers to buyers so we have no physical location.

What sets your organic food store apart from others? +

Our store is distinguished by its unwavering commitment to trust, quality, and sustainability. We prioritize 100% organic products, work with responsible producers, and are dedicated to reducing our environmental impact.

Do you offer delivery options, and what is the delivery process? +

Yes, we offer convenient delivery options. Simply select your products, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Our efficient delivery team ensures your organic goodies are safely delivered to your doorstep, with options for tracking your order.

How can I stay updated on special offers and new arrivals? +

To stay informed about our latest promotions, new arrivals, and organic food tips, consider subscribing to our newsletter. You'll receive regular updates directly in your inbox, so you never miss out on exciting offers and fresh, seasonal products.

Do you offer bulk purchasing options for businesses or larger quantities? +

Yes, we do offer bulk purchasing options for businesses, restaurants, and individuals looking to buy in larger quantities. Please contact our sales team through our website, and they will assist you in sourcing the quantity you need at competitive prices.
